## 5. Platform Neutrality

OpenSeal is not bound to a specific platform (e.g., HighStation).

However:

*   The **Execution Environment generating proofs** using OpenSeal may be a platform-specific implementation.
*   Such implementations are not included in the OpenSeal open source scope.

ðŸ“Œ *OpenSeal is the Standard, Platform is a Choice.*
